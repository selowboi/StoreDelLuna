# Store Del Luna

Store Del Luna is a K-Pop merchandise supplier that provides original K-Pop albums and merchandises from many artists. This project was made by me and my friends (Benny Kharisma and Andre Christian) and was assigned to us during our fourth semester at Bina Nusantara University. The purpose of this project is to test our knowledge about Photoshop, Web-Design, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. We managed to finished the project before the due time and got a an A for this assignment.
